The Wedding Day

All the day's activities take place at Carberry Tower Mansion House. See the map below for location.

1pm: Guests Arrive

The bar will be open so arrive from 1pm onwards and enjoy a drink before we get started.

2pm: The Ceremony

We will be starting sharp at 2pm, the Master of Ceremony will lead you to the chapel.

5pm: The Reception

We will line your stomach with food and the wedding party will regale you with speeches.

7pm: The Party

The evening guests arrive and the ceilidh band will help us party until midnight.

Accommodation

Premier Inn

The Permier Inn Mussleburgh is 20 minutes walk from Carberry Tower Mansion House. You can book on their website or phone them on 0871 527 8358. Contact us if you have any issues.

Edinburgh Hotels

Edinburgh City Centre is a 30 minute taxi ride from Carberry Tower Mansion House. There are many hotel options on TripAdvisor. We recommend prebooking with a Private Hire company to get the best fare.
