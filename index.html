The Wedding Day

All the day's activities take place at Carberry Tower Mansion House. See the map below for location.

1pm: Guests Arrive

The bar will be open so arrive from 1pm onwards and enjoy a drink before we get started.

2pm: The Ceremony

We will be starting sharp at 2pm, the Master of Ceremony will lead you to the chapel.

5pm: The Reception

We will line your stomach with food and the wedding party will regale you with speeches.

7pm: The Party

The evening guests arrive and the ceilidh band will help us party until midnight.
